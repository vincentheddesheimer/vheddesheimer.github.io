Curriculum Vitae

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Politics at Princeton University. My research lies at the intersection of Comparative Politics and Political Economy. I study working on inequality related to ownership of financial assets, housing financialization, and monetary policy. I am studying these topics using formal theory and paying close attention to causal identification in quasi-experimental settings.
